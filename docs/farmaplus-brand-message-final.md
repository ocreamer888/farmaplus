# FarmaPlus Brand Message Final (Bilingual)

## 1) Strategic Direction Approved

- Blend selected: **Approach B + C**
- Core idea:
  - Continuity of care ("salud sin pausa")
  - Innovation + neuro-design mindset + agile, modern service
- Public: **Mixed** (local families, older adults/caregivers, tourists/foreign residents)
- Tone: **Balanced** (warm + professional)
- Language: **Bilingual** (Spanish + English)
- Constraint applied: no absolute emergency promise in primary message

---

## 2) Core Brand Message (Official)

### English (EN)

**FarmaPlus: uninterrupted care, powered by intelligent care. In Huacas, Guanacaste, we connect telemedicine, 24/7 pharmacy, and express pharmacy to deliver agile, modern, and human support.**

## 7) Brand Voice Guide (Short)

### 7.1 Brand Essence

- ES: **Salud sin pausa con cuidado inteligente, humano y agil.**
- EN: **Uninterrupted care with intelligent, human, and agile support.**

### 7.2 Personality

- Cercana
- Profesional
- Moderna
- Resolvedora

### 7.3 Tone of Voice

- Sounds like: clear, warm, direct, trustworthy
- Avoid: cold, complex, exaggerated, aggressive language

### 7.4 Keywords to Use

- ES: confianza, rapido, acompanado, conectado, cuidado, 24/7, express, orientacion medica
- EN: trust, fast, supported, connected, care, 24/7, express, medical guidance

### 7.5 Messaging Guardrails

- Avoid absolute promises that are not operationally verified
- Avoid unnecessary technical jargon
- Avoid fear-based persuasion
